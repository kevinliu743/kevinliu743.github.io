ABOUT ME

ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Kevin.

My high school chemistry teacher calls me Renaissance Man; my mother suggests that I'm lazy. My peers say I'm smart, but I like to think of myself as just me: Kevin. I am talented in many things, and I love to explore. Sure, I might not shoot like Steph Curry, but give me a ball and I’ll put it in the basket. I might not have really coded before college, but give me a year at UC Berkeley and I will excel. I may never be able to invest like Warren Buffet, but I’m learning to invest in a way that works best for me.

Throughout my life, there have been very few things that I was not good at. As long as I put my mind to it, I found that I really could do anything, and do them well. So for that reason, I never shy away from challenges; rather, I enjoy them and meet them head-first, understanding that the experience will only help me improve.

Every day, I have the same goals in mind: Learn, Improve, and Make a Difference. Whether it’s honing by professional skills, reading for knowledge, or making someone smile, all I want is to make every day count. I believe that we are all here for a reason, and life is too short to not progress. I cherish each and every moment of whatever I do, and knowing that I’ve done my best allows me to live my life with fewer regrets.

I am proud of my past, but I am even more excited about my future. There are so many places, activities, and experiences to explore and I can’t wait for what’s next...
